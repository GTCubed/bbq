Texas-Style BBQ, Done Right

Welcome to Come and Taste It BBQ! My name is Greg, and I am Denton’s newest pitmaster. After spending over 16 years in an unfulfilling IT role, I knew there was something better out there for me. My journey into the world of BBQ began when I helped a friend with his food truck and participated in charity BBQ cook-offs. It was during these experiences that I discovered my true passion: making great BBQ for others to enjoy. In the summer of 2022, I bought my first food truck and transformed it into the purple beast you see now. Every element of this truck reflects my dedication and love for BBQ. I am thrilled to share my passion with you all. It’s been a long road to get here, but it’s been worth every moment. I hope to see you soon and share some delicious BBQ with you!
